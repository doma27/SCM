Battery Charging

§  Switch on the charger with the SCM off

§  When the charger is switched on the red neon inside the power switch will illuminate

§  If the charger current is above 300mA the green ‘charged’ LED will be illuminated.

§  When the charger current is below 300mA the green ‘charged’ LED will be off.

§  The charger should be left on and connected to the SCM.

§  If required, the battery charger may be used as the power supply if the battery is disconnected.

§  If the green LED does not light within eight hours, the serviceability of the batteries should be checked.

Important Warning Messages

To ensure that the condition of the spare battery pack does not deteriorate through lack of use, the battery pack in the SCM should be exchanged with the spare battery pack, if supplied, at 4 monthly intervals or charged using the battery box charging cable supplied with spare batteries if supplied.
