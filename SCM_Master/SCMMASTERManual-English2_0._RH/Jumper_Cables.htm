Jumper Cables

Each transducer on board the SCM has an M8 socket connection fitted.

These cables provide the connection between the transducers M8 socket and the distribution blocks.


The male end of each cable is a fitted rubber ‘O’ ring which creates a water tight seal when making connections.

Figure 37: Jumper Cables

Each cable is pre-moulded and come in a number of sizes depending on the location within the SCM.

Each customer will have different sizes of cables fitted depending on the size of their SCM.
These cable lengths can be found in your “Parts List” section of the manual.

Further information can also be found out by contacting Sarclad direct.
