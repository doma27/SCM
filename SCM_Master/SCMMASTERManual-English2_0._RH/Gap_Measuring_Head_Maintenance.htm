Gap Measuring Head Maintenance

This procedure details dismantling and replacement of the assembly in case of regular maintenance or of failure:

1.     1.    Remove the four screws securing the measuring head assembly to the SCM.

2.     2.    Withdraw and dry the assembly and release the connector.

3.     3.   Â Release and withdraw the gap sensor Sensors that are used to measure the roll gaps, and outer roll condition by removing the four screws.

4.     4.    Clean the inside of the measuring head - do not grease

Reconstruction is the reverse procedure to dismantling taking note of the precautions detailed in on the drawings.

When replacing the assembly, make sure that the SCM thickness holes in the flange are in line with the hole in the flange seat on the SCM and that you can see a clear path through the SCM body.

Ensure the longer stroke sensor is replaced on the inner face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Inner Face Rolls, may also be referred to as the loose or adjustable face. and that the shorter sensor is on the outer face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Outer Face Rolls, may also be referred to as fixed or back face..

The sensor, which was extracted, should now be calibrated, followed by SCM thickness according to the calibration section.
