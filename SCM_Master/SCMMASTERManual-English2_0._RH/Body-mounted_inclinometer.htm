Body-mounted inclinometer Devices that measure angles relative to the horizontal

1.    Locate the body mounted inclinometer and note its orientation.

2.    Unfasten the connector.

3.    Unscrew the inclinometer and bracket together, from the SCM body.

4.    Clean the bracket contact surface thoroughly, ensure it is perfectly flat and mount it to the calibration bracket on the rotary table.

5.    Ensure that the inclinometer is mounted onto the bracket in the correct orientation.

6.    Re-Connect the inclinometer connector.
