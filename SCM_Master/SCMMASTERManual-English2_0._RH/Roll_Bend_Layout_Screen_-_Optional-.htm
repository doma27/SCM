Roll Bend Layout Screen - Optional

The individual roll bend readings are shown on the roll configurations. The position of the data on the roll is representative of the actual location of where the measurements are taken by the columns of bend sensors.

The bend value is determined by measuring the roll gap, at different points on the circumference, whilst the rolls are rotated. The different measurements of the same roll are then compared. The difference between the maximum and minimum measured gap values is the value of bend that is displayed.

The colour of the bar graph depends on the acceptance thresholds values. Results within threshold are shown in green; results above the upper threshold level are shown in red.

This data can also be viewed as a graph.
