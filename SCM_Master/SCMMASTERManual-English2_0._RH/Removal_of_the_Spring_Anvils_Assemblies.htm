Important Warning Messages

The spring anvil assemblies are heavy. We recommended that two operators remove heavy items from the SCM body.

1.  Clean the anvil retention keys of solid debris.

2.    Insert the spring compressor rods through the anvil and screw in to the SCM body until full thread engagement is achieved as per drawing B -14 - 01.

3.    Fit the anvil compressor washer and nut.

4.    Using a spanner on the end of each rod to prevent the rod from turning, tighten the nuts in turn to compress the anvil sufficiently to release the spring load on the anvil keys. This should be at least 1 mm.

 

 

5.    Remove the anvil key retaining screws and the anvil retaining keys.

6.    Remove the spherical collar cover plate, (Only applicable for SCMs with hydraulics)

7.    Remove the locking washer and unscrew the spherical collar from the hydraulic cylinder using the hydraulic wrenches to prevent the cylinder shaft from rotating (Only applicable for SCMs with hydraulics).

8.    Using a spanner on the end of each compressor rod to prevent the rod from turning, slacken the nuts in turn until the spring loading is completely released.

 

9.    Remove the nuts and washers, but leave the compressor rods in place.
