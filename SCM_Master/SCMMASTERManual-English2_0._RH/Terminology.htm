Terminology

Term

Description

SCM

Strand Condition Monitor.

Left

Left when viewed from the mould in the casting direction.

Right

Right when viewed from the mould in the casting direction.

Outer rolls

Identified as the rolls in the caster whose radius is greater than that of the Inner face rolls (or “fixed side”).

Inner rolls

Identified as the rolls in the caster whose radius is less than that of the Outer face rolls (or “loose side”).

SCM outer face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Outer Face Rolls, may also be referred to as fixed or back face.

The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Outer Face Rolls, may also be referred to as fixed or back face.

SCM inner face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Inner Face Rolls, may also be referred to as the loose or adjustable face.

The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Inner Face Rolls, may also be referred to as the loose or adjustable face.

Slab thickness The nominal thickness of steel being cast.

Refers to the nominal thickness of steel being cast.

SCM profile thickness

The thickness of the SCM body.

SCM flange-to-flange thickness

The distance between the flanges of the inner and outer face gap sensors.

Full length rolls

Where there is only one roll across the caster width.

Split rolls Where there are two (or more) rolls across the width of the caster at each roll position.

Where there are two (or more) rolls across the width of the caster at each roll position.
