Water Spray Threshold Summary Screen

The water spray threshold summary screen provides a quick overview of water spray results for the caster with reference to the threshold limit.

The water spray results are separated into inner and outer face data for ease of analysis.

Results within the acceptance thresholds are shown in blue.

Results below the threshold level are shown in red, so indicating hot areas in the caster where there is little or no water spray cooling.

The acceptance threshold can be set from 0 to 100 in steps of 1.
