Trolley Mounted Hydraulic Power Unit (HPU)

The reservoir must be filled through the breather hole provided, with oil to the correct specification, as the filler has an integral strainer to remove any contamination in the new fluid.

NOTE: Do not fill the reservoir beyond maximum level mark on sight-glass situated on the end of the reservoir (reservoir capacity is 15 litres).

The pack is now ready to be used, during initial operation a check should be made on system pressure, filter condition and temperature.

The system pressure can be read directly from the gauge on the tank lid. During these first hours of use if you do notice any leaks, repair them immediately; the most common fix required is to re-tighten the connectors or bolts.
