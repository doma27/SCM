The inclinometer Devices that measure angles relative to the horizontal system is very sensitive to movement from the SCM. Dirt or scale in contact with the measuring blade causes misleading results that can often be identified and ignored.

In the example at roll positions 2-3-4 and 3-4-5, the step errors are -1.0 mm and +1.0 mm. This is due to the incorrect angle measured whilst the blade was not in contact with the steel roll surface at roll 3 as the angle across 3 and 4 was measured. The error is characterised by a large negative value followed by a large positive.

Care is always required to interpret the differences between invalid results due to dirt and segment misalignment results.

Other displays such as gaps will add to the decision process in this case.
