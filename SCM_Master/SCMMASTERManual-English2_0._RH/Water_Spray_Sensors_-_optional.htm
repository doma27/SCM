Water Spray Sensors - optional

Water spray sensors are used to measure the condition of the water spray system.

Figure 5: Water Spray Sensor Sensors that are used to measure the condition of the water spray system - the sensor output being proportional to the amount and velocity of water being sprayed onto the detection membrane

The sensor output is proportional to the amount and velocity of water being sprayed onto the detection membrane. The output is an analogue voltage between 0v (no spray) to approximately 10v (maximum spray).

The water spray sensors are powered by a 12v regulated supply.

All water spray outputs are fed directly to the single board computer (SBC Single-board computer) through the distribution blocks.
