Mobile Hydraulic Power Unit – Only applicable for top feed SCMs

The hydraulic power unit is built into a trolley which may be easily moved by an operator.

This unit should be connected to a convenient mains power supply located close to the SCM anvil retraction position (this will normally be close to the chain carriage or in the stand).

The power unit connects to the SCM via a hose and quick release fitting.

When mains power is connected and the green ON button is pressed the pump will start.

When the valve lever on the HPU is actuated the hydraulic oil is pumped to the SCM hydraulics and the cylinder pressure will increase, which will retract the SCM anvils.
