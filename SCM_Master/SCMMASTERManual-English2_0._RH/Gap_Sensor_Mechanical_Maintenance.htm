Gap Sensor Mechanical Maintenance

NOTE: Gap sensor cleaning maintenance should be carried out every year or fifty measurements or sooner if required.

With the sensor removed dismantle the device, by holding the sensor on the flats of the threaded end washer, remove the domed locknut then remove the threaded end washer and finally the spring.

Important Warning Messages

Beware of the spring’s force

Inspect and clean thoroughly with water ensuring that all dirt and scale is removed.

Replace the spring, followed by the threaded washer and domed locknut, ensuring there is smooth movement of the shaft.

Replace the sensor as detailed in section below.
