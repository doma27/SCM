Blade-mounted inclinometers

1.    Unfasten the SCM end plate screws securing the inclinometer Devices that measure angles relative to the horizontal assemblies to the SCM body.

2.    Carefully withdraw the end plate far enough to give access to the inclinometer connector.

3.    Unfasten the connector.

4.    Completely remove the end plate / inclinometer blade assembly.

5.    Remove the inclinometer blade from its mounting plate, taking care not to misplace any washers or spacers.

6.    Clean the blade contact surface thoroughly, ensure it is perfectly flat and mount it to the calibration bracket on the rotary table.

7.    Ensure that the inclinometer is mounted onto the bracket in the correct orientation.

8.    (Left Left when viewed from the mould in the casting direction. side inclinometer mounted with the connector toward the operator, the right Right when viewed from the mould in the casting direction. side inclinometer mounted with the connector away from the operator.)

9.    Re-connect the inclinometer connector.
