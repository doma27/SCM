Batteries

The battery pack is housed in a stainless steel, free venting enclosure. It is attached to the SCM by four screws under a cover plate (looking on the inner face of the SCM).

Figure 38: Battery Pack

12V lead/acid sealed batteries are connected in parallel to supply 12V to the SCM providing approximately 8+ hours of operation before recharging is required.

The battery level may be checked by selection of the  BAtt    option from the test menu (this test should be performed at least two minutes after switch on of the SCM).

i
Important Information

When the SCM is not in use the batteries should be kept charged to maximum capacity by permanent connection to the battery charger.
