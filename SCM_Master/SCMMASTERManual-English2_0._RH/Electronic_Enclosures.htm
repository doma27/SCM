Electronic Enclosures

There is one main electronic enclosure in the SCM. This houses the 64-Channel Data Logger PCB Printed Circuit Board (single board computer (SBC Single-board computer).

If a customer has chosen the WIFI option, there will be another enclosure fitted within the SCM; this will house the serial to wireless transmitter.

All transducers are connected to the distribution blocks via the M8 connection leads.
