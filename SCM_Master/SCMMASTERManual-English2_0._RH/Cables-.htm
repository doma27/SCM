Cables

Cables are provided for communication, calibration and battery charging.

The main cables provided for the system are:

  • The cable for communication between the laptop computer and the SCM

  • The battery charging cable


The inclinometer, gap and water sensor are connected into distribution blocks via pre-moulded 3 pin M8 jumper cables. The distribution blocks are hard wired into the data logger enclosure.

 

The internal cables for the system are listed in the parts list which will be with this manual.

All cables can be replaced if they get damaged by contacting our sales team.
