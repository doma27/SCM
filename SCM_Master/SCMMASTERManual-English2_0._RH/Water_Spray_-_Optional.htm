Water Spray - Optional

As the water spray sensors pass through the spray jets, the sensors output a varying voltage to the on board computer. This voltage increases proportionally to the increasing impact of the water sprays.

The sensor output will increase as the sensors reach the centre of the spray and decrease as the spray is passed.

The computer will store the maximum output detected throughout the spray area.

If comparison of spray results is required from measurement to measurement, the identical caster spray set-up should be selected each time. If a different caster spray configuration is used, comparison across the slab and between rolls will still be valid but measurement to measurement comparisons will not be valid. This display only gives an indication of the presence of water and is not a measurement of the volume or pressure of water being sprayed.
