Distribution Blocks

Each distribution block is wired into the Data Logger enclosure and terminated into the 64-Channel Data Logger PCB.

The Data Logger PCB then distributes +12v, -12v and +5v to the on-board electrical sensors via these blocks.

Figure 35: Distribution blocks for connecting Data Logger to electrical sensors

The distribution blocks come in 4 different types:

  • M8 6-Way Distribution Block

  • M8 8-Way Distribution Block

  • M8 10-Way Distribution Block

  • M8 12-Way Distribution Block

 

They provide a quick easy way to interchange components on the SCM without the need for re-wiring.

For the channels on the blocks which are not in use, a water-proof cap should be fitted to prevent water ingress.
