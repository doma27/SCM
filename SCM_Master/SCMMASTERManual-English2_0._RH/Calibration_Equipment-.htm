Calibration Equipment

The range of calibration equipment supplied enables full calibration / test of the SCM to be performed.

Typical calibration equipment supplied consists of the following:

A digital depth gauge (DDG) and a set of rods whose length is set specifically for each SCM.

  • A calibrat77ed gauging sleeve that enables a calibrated pre-set point to be set on the digital depth gauge.

  • A gauging ring(s) to enable correct seating of the digital depth gauge onto Gap sensor flange during SCM thickness measurement.

  • A set of calibration caps to define two points on the straight-line characteristic of the Gap sensors on the outer and inner faces respectively.

  • A rotary table with fixing bracket and digital spirit gauge for positioning the rotary table during inclinometer calibration.
