Articulated Spring Anvil Maintenance 

The following procedure gives details of how to dismantle and replace the assembly in case of failure or excessive build-up of debris in the spring cavity.

Strong sets of spring anvils, each with spring cassettes are fitted to the SCM body inner face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Inner Face Rolls, may also be referred to as the loose or adjustable face..

Weaker sets, each with smaller spring cassettes are fitted to the outer face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Outer Face Rolls, may also be referred to as fixed or back face..

Important Warning Messages

Great care should be taken when dismantling the spring anvils due to the large spring forces present.

The spring anvil system requires no normal maintenance.

 Important Information

To minimise the number of parts removed from the SCM at any time, the sequence has been split into separate parts. Removal and replacement of a single anvil at a time should be adopted as the preferred procedure.
