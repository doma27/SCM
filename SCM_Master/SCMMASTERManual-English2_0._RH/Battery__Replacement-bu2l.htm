Important Warning Messages

The cover plate and batteries are heavy.

To replace the battery, take the following steps:

  • Disconnect the battery and charger.

  • Remove the cover plate (when looking on the inner face) and disconnect the in-line Fischer battery connector.

  • Unfasten the securing screws and remove the battery pack.

  • To replace the batteries, follow the above steps in reverse order.

  • Re-connect the battery and charger.

Important Information

Any old batteries should be discarded.

The box and connector should be retained for spares.

The spare battery box (if supplied) will already have batteries wired in parallel and terminals sealed with silicone rubber prior to installation.
