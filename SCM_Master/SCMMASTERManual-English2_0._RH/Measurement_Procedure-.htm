Measurement Procedure

Setting the DDG with the Gauging Sleeve

  • Place the gauging sleeve on a flat, level surface (the top of the SCM is usually a good place).

  • Insert the DDG into the sleeve

  • Hold down the sleeve with the finger grips

  • Press the <SET> button TWICE until it displays the set thickness.

Figure 21: Setting the Digital Depth Gauge

The DDG is now set correctly to measure the thickness.

Measuring the thickness

  • Clean the measuring head flanges ensuring the surfaces are smooth and free from damage.

  • Connect the laptop computer to the SCM.

  • Switch the SCM on and ensure the Interface displays are ready.

  • Select the thickness calibration button on the laptop computer, from the calibration menu.

  • Fasten the deepest calibration cap to the flange of the outer face sensor position to be measured.

  • Place the appropriate SCM thickness gauging ring over the flange of the inner face sensor position to be measured. Ensure that the hole in the gauging ring is in line with the hole in the sensor flange.

  • Insert the DDG rod through the hole in the ring and sensor flange until the rod presses against the calibration cap on the other face. Ensuring that all surfaces mate and the plunger on the DDG is fully down, enter the reading seen on the DDG into the laptop computer.

 

  • Repeat for the other sensor pairs (as required).

  • When all the readings have been collected, ensure the readings have been entered on the laptop computer and the data is transferred back to the SCM before exiting the calibration menu.
