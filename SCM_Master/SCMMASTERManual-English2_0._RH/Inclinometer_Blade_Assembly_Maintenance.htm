Inclinometer Devices that measure angles relative to the horizontal Blade Assembly Maintenance

The following procedure describes how to dismantle and replace the assembly in case of failure or excessive build-up of debris in the inclinometer cavity.

The sensors are mounted on accurately machined blade assemblies situated at each end of the SCM body.

Important Warning Messages

Take due care when lifting. Heavy weights are involved in this procedure.

The dismantling procedure is as follows:

1.     Unfasten the SCM end plate screws securing the inclinometer assembly to the SCM body.

2.     Carefully withdraw the end plate / inclinometer assembly far enough to give access to the inclinometer bayonet connector.

3.     Unfasten the bayonet connector.

4.     Completely remove the end plate / inclinometer blade assembly.

5.     Remove the plain washers and guide spacers.

6.     Unfasten the extension springs from the spring pillars on the end plate.

7.     Remove the inclinometer blade, taking care not to misplace any washers or spacers.

8.     When the system has been dismantled, examine all parts for damage and replace if necessary.

9.     Replacement is the reverse of removal after cleaning all sliding surfaces and smearing threads in copper anti-seize grease.

10.  Replace the assembly onto the SCM being careful not to trap any cables.
