Other Hardware Problems

 

 

Fault

Possible Cause

Tests to Perform

1.             

Data not present for all rolls of the caster.

SCM has powered off Measurement has exceeded 4 hours.

Repeat measurement.

 

Faulty power supply.

2.             

Downloaded data not available for viewing i.e. data analysis fails.

A caster measurement has not been performed.

Perform a real measurement.

Faulty or out of calibration inclinometer Devices that measure angles relative to the horizontal.

Check condition of inclinometer blades and calibration of Inclinometers sensors.

Faulty or out of calibration gap sensor Sensors that are used to measure the roll gaps, and outer roll condition.

Check condition of gap sensor arms and calibration of gap sensors.

Data measurement has terminated prematurely.

 

3.             

Cable communications cannot be established to the laptop.

Damaged communications cable.

Check connectors and cable for damage.

Incorrect Com port settings.

Check com port is set correctly in the .INI file using Device Manager.

Unavailable com1 port.

Check laptop Com1 port settings, Uninstall any recently installed 3rd party software from laptop.

Loose connections

Check all connections from the Comms cable through to PCB Printed Circuit Board P2A

4.             

LED display is corrupt.

Loose connector.

Check the main connectors on SBC Single-board computer card P2A/B.

Faulty display.

 

Recycle power to display. If problem persists change display and / or SBC.

5.             

No output from gap sensor(s).

Blown fuse.

Check for presence of voltages on SBC using the tables in this manual for correct voltages.

Loose connector on SBC

Check connector on SBC PCB.

Damaged connector or cabling.

Check all cables and connectors into distribution blocks.

6.             

No output from inclinometer sensor(s).

Blown fuse(s).

Check for presence of voltages on SBC using the tables in this manual for correct voltages.

Failed sensor

Replace with new

Damaged connector or cabling.

Check connector P2 on SARDAQ board.

7.             

Suspect gap data.

Damaged sensor.

Inspect sensor and housing for physical damage.

Open sensor housing and inspect for water ingress.

Damaged connectors or cabling.

Inspect cabling for signs of physical damage.

Open sensor connectors and inspect for signs of water ingress.

Out of calibration.

Recalibrate gap sensor.

Dirt in sensor housing

Open sensor and clean thoroughly

Failing gap sensor

Replace with new

8.             

Suspect inclinometer data.

Damaged sensor.

Inspect inclinometer and blade for physical damage.

Inspect cabling for signs of physical damage.

Damaged connectors or cabling.

Inspect cabling for signs of physical damage.

Open sensor connectors and inspect for signs of water ingress.

Out of calibration.

Recalibrate inclinometer sensor.

Failing sensor

Replace with new
