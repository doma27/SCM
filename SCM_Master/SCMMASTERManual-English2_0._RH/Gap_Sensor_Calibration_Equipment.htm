Gap Sensor Calibration Equipment

The following items are required for Gap sensor calibration:

§  Calibration cap to calibrate outer face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Outer Face Rolls, may also be referred to as fixed or back face. sensors.

§  Calibration cap to calibrate inner face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Inner Face Rolls, may also be referred to as the loose or adjustable face. sensors.

§  Laptop computer.

§  Laptop computer to SCM data transfer cable.
