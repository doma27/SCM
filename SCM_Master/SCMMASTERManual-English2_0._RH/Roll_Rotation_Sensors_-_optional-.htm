Roll Rotation Sensors - optional

The roll rotation sensors detect how freely the rolls in the caster are rotating. This reading is only an indication as an absolute measurement is not possible.

Figure 4: Roll Rotation Sensor

The roll rotation sensor incorporates a proximity switch to detect the roll and another proximity switch to record the rotation of the roll.

The proximity switches are powered by the +12v regulated supply and their outputs are either approximately 11v (HIGH) when in the proximity of metal or 0v (LOW) when not.

All proximity switch outputs are fed directly to the single board computer (SBC) through the distribution blocks.
