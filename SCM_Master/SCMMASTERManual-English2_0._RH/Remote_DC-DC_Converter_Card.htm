Remote DC-DC Converter Card

Mounted behind the 64-Channel Data Logger PCB Printed Circuit Board (single board computer) is the DC converter card. This converts the raw 12 volt battery supply to the regulated +12v, -12V and +5V supplies required by the system.

Thermal compound is applied onto the DC-DC converters and the PCB is secured into the enclosure with 6 screws. The enclosure acts as a heat sink to dissipate the heat generated by the DC-DC converters.

Figure 33: DC Converter Card

The card is connected directly into the single board computer (SBC).
