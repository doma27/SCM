Changing SCM Thickness - Optional

i
 Important Information

The main reason for enhancing the monitor thickness is to allow the gap sensors to measure multiple gap ranges.

Adding enhancement plates allows the same sensors to measure multiple slab thicknesses without changing any electrical components.

The SCM thickness conversion consists of three stages:

1.     8.    Increasing or reducing the distance between the gap sensors to position them within the correct measuring range.

2.     9.    Adding or removing the inner face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Inner Face Rolls, may also be referred to as the loose or adjustable face. anvil enhancement plates to enable the spring anvils to interact with the rolls in the designed manner for the thicker / thinner option respectively.

NOTE: When removing and refitting enhancement plates make sure that you have placed the SCM in the horizontal attitude with its inner face uppermost.

3.     10. Re-calibrating the gap sensors and the SCM flange-to-flange thickness The distance between the flanges of the inner and outer face gap sensors.:

§  Remove all inner gap sensors by extracting the four cap head retaining screws and all inner roll rotation units (if fitted) releasing the in-line connectors noting their original positions.

§  Remove the Inner face spring anvil assemblies - see the section on Removal of the Spring Anvils Assemblies.

§  Ensure that the SCM body surfaces and all enhancement plates are clean.

§  In the case of the various different thicknesses the mounting plates and anvils need to be assembled as detailed in drawings.

§  Replace the Inner face spring anvil assemblies - see the section on Replacement of the Spring Anvils.

§  Replace the gap sensors and roll rotation sensors in their original positions.

The SCM thickness must be measured and the gap sensors calibrated.

Transfer the SCM thickness and new calibration to the PC prior to a measurement run.

Adding the Spring Anvil Enhancement Plates

As well as moving the gap and bend sensors, we also have to add plates to the anvils to allow correct operation.

This can be done by fixing the extra plates to the anvils. In the example pictured below the following measurements would be possible:

§  With no anvil plates added, the SCM can measure 200mm,

§  with the red plates 225mm can be measured

§  with the blue plates added, 250mm slab thickness can be measured.

The screws used to attach the anvil enhancement plates are M12 x 25mm hexagon socket. These screws are used for both 225mm and 250mm enhancements.

To check the necessary tightness of each screw, refer to the appendix on Torque Settings at the end of the manual.
