Preparing the Digital Depth Gauge (DDG)

 

Fit the battery

 

When the battery is fitted the DDG should look like this

 

Press the <SET> twice

 

Press the <MODE> - set mm

Press and hold <MODE> - display flashes

 

 

 

Press <SET> - display stops flashing

Press <MODE> - reverse mode appears

 

 

 

Press <SET> - sets reverse mode

Press and hold <MODE>

 

Setting the pre-set value

This example will show a pre-set of 218.00mm

To determine the correct pre-set value, use the following calculation:-

Pre-set value = gauging sleeve – gauging ring

 

Press <SET> - P flashes

Press and hold <SET> - + flashes

Press <SET> - 0 flashes

 

 

Press <SET> until correct digit flashes

 

Press <MODE> twice – increments the digit to 2

 

 

Press <SET> - next digit flashes

 

Press <MODE> - increases the flashing digit to 1

 

 

Press <SET> - next digit flashes

 

Press <MODE> eight times – increments the digit to 8

 

 

Press <SET> until P flashes

 

Press <SET> - the display should now show

 

 

Screw the DDG rods together
