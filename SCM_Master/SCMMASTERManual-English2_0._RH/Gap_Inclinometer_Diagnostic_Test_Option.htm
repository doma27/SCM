Gap & Inclinometer Devices that measure angles relative to the horizontal Diagnostic Test Option

Selection of the _diag    option from the test menu allows the user to monitor the rest levels of gap and inclinometer sensors. On entering this menu, the system is scanned and these  output levels are compared to the levels recorded during the last calibration performed and any gap sensors that change by more than +/- 0.5mm are displayed for a few seconds e.g. tx 001  , 15 58  followed by any inclinometers that are more than +/- 1 degree from 90 degrees e.g. , 88 40  assuming the SCM is placed vertically in the stand.

If all levels are within tolerance the display will show _cal ok ,  rest    good   followed by  inc     good    the operator will then be returned to top level menu showing  diag .
