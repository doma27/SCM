Procedure for a Caster Measurement

The SCM is fitted in place of the dummy bar head on the dummy bar chain.

For bottom feed casters the SCM is switched on prior to insertion into the caster.

For top feed systems the SCM must first have its anvils retracted so that it may pass through the mould. Once through the mould the anvils are released and are no longer required. (Refer to section 4.3 for details on anvil operation.)

For either measurement option the chain and SCM should be fed into the caster in the usual way, smoothly and at any speed, until the SCM is in the correct starting position. (The SCM can start at any position in the caster.)

The SCM is then switched on to start recording data. On pressing the record button on the SCM the system starts gathering and logging data. All data is stored in the On-Board computer as the SCM and chain travels through the caster.

In the case of an up measurement the SCM should not be driven any higher than the safest highest roll position as detailed in the drawings to prevent any damage to the caster or mould.

The chain and SCM can be stopped at any position within the caster and its direction reversed to log data in the opposite direction of travel.

When it has completed its travel through the caster the data can be transferred by connecting the laptop via the communications cable on com port 1 to the connector on the SCM body.
