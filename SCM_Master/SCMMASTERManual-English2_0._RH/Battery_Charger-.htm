Battery Charger

The battery charger ensures that the on-board batteries are fully charged.

The battery charger is a self-regulating 12v device with a charging current of up to 3A.

The charger senses the charged condition of the battery pack. A green LED indicates the battery is charging; when the green LED turns off the battery is trickle charged to continuously keep it at the optimum capacity. The Power ON/OFF switch illuminates when the charger is connected to the mains supply and switched on.

The green LED will only illuminate if the battery is connected and needs charging. It will not illuminate if the battery is not connected to the charger or it is at full capacity.
