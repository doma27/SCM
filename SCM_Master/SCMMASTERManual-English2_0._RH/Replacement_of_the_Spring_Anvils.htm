Important Warning Messages

When re-assembling the anvils, ensure that the springs are seated correctly before proceeding with spring compression.

1.    Slide the cups (if fitted) and springs onto the compressor rods followed by the spring anvil assembly.

2.    Replace and tighten the compression washers and nuts until the level of the retaining slots on the anvil are at least 1mm below the level of the retaining key seats.

3.    Replace and fully tighten the anvil retaining keys ensuring that all screws are smeared with anti-seize grease prior to fitting.

4.    Refit the spherical collar to the hydraulic cylinder shaft using the hydraulic wrenches to prevent the cylinder shaft from rotating (Only applicable for SCMs with Hydraulics).

5.    Refit the locking washer and spherical collar cover plate (Only applicable for SCMs with Hydraulics).

6.    Using a spanner on the end of each compressor rod in turn, to prevent the rod from turning, undo the nut on each of the anvil compressors evenly to release the load back onto the retaining keys.

7.    Withdraw the compressor rods.
