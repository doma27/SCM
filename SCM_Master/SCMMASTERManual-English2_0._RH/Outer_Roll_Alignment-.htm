Outer Roll Alignment

Outer face roll alignment shows the shape of the outer face of the caster in terms of a step error between a roll and its two adjacent rolls.

Any rolls that cause a discontinuity in the curve above a pre-set threshold are highlighted.

This is achieved by measuring the angle of two adjacent rolls on both the right and left-hand sides of the caster, as the inclinometer blade assemblies lie across them in a stable attitude.

The angle at this exact position is measured many times over a time window. The measured data is then analysed and the exact angle determined; it is this angle that is then stored in the on-board computer.
