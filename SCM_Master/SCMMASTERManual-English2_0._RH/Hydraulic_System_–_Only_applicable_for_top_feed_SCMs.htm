Hydraulic System – Only applicable for top feed SCMs


The SCM hydraulic system is used to retract the spring anvils in order to fit the SCM through the mould without damage to either.

The system consists of a free venting oil system with hydraulic cylinders which are mounted behind the spring anvil cassettes. A solenoid valve is used to energise or de-energise the hydraulic system and a pressure switch to indicate when the correct hydraulic pressure has been reached and the spring anvils are fully retracted.

Externally a mobile power unit provides the hydraulic pressure source. The hydraulic power unit connects to the SCM via a quick release connector.

When the solenoid valve is energised via the remote control, a flashing led indicator on the Interface unit is displayed. The external hydraulic power unit is then connected and operated to increase the hydraulic pressure in the cylinders. Once the cylinders are at the correct pressure and the anvils are fully retracted the power unit is no longer required and is disconnected from the SCM.

A second LED on the interface unit will indicate when the operating pressure has been obtained. This pressure is pre-set before shipping of the SCM from the UK.

When the solenoid valve is released the anvils are returned to their extended position under the spring anvil force and the excess hydraulic oil is vented. Both LEDs will then be extinguished.
