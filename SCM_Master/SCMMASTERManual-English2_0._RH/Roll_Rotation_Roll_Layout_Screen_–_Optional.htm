Roll Rotation Roll Layout Screen  – Optional

This is an optional screen that shows the rotation condition of both the inner and outer face The face of the SCM that is in contact with the Outer Face Rolls, may also be referred to as fixed or back face. rolls at each roll location.

The individual readings are shown on the roll configurations. The position of the data on the roll is representative of the actual location where the measurements are taken.

The sensor numbers are identified at the top of the page and the results show a difference between the speed that the SCM passes the roll and the rotation speed of the roll

The rotating force is provided by the spring anvils on either face of the SCM.

A freely rotating roll would give an indication of 0% with no corresponding graph, whilst a seized roll that has seized up would give an indication of 100% and a corresponding large vertical bar graph.

Any intermediate result will fall between these limits.

Results below the acceptance level are shown in green, whilst those above are shown in red.

The acceptance threshold can be set from 0 to 100% in steps of 1%. A typical threshold would be 60%.

A graphical display of the roll rotation results can also be viewed.
