Power Related Problems

 

Fault

Possible Cause

Tests to Perform

SCM will not power on and the Green Power ON LED on the display is not present.

Faulty IR transmitter.

Check battery on IR Transmitter. Try spare IR transmitter.

Faulty IR receiver.

Check that IR received LED is indicating.

See if the SBC can be booted by operating the by-pass toggle switch on the card (SW1)

SCM battery fault.

Check LED D18 on SBC, Check local battery fuses. Connect charger and turn on. Change battery if problem persists.

Faulty SBC.

Replace SBC

SCM is powered off when exiting the caster.

Flat battery.

Amber LED is illuminated.

Connect charger and download latest run.

Measurement has exceeded 4.5 hrs.

View battery voltage of latest measurement on PC display. Time of measurement is displayed on X axis of display.
