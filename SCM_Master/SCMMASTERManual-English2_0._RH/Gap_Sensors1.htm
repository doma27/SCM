Gap Sensors

To measure the roll gap and outer roll condition (ORC), gap sensors are used to produce an output proportional to the amount that the domed cap of the measuring head is depressed.

The accuracy of these devices permits the roll gap to be measured to an accuracy of +/- 0.1mm, which can be proved under calibration conditions.
