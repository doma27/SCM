Loading a Measurement

Once the caster measurement data has been transferred and stored in the main computer memory results can be displayed on the screen.

From the VIEW MEASUREMENT DATA screen select the LOAD MEASURING TRIAL button  to load the measurement selection screen.

On this screen a complete list of the saved measurements is displayed in the left hand table.

The table shows the date and time a measurement was performed, the direction of the measurement, the measurement number and the measurement comments entered at the time of data transfer.

Every time a new measurement is performed the measurement number is automatically incremented. The measurement numbers are unique and no two measurements will have the same number.

Using the mouse pointer, highlight the desired measurement on the left hand table and then load the measurement into the first position of the right-hand table by pressing the Load Measurement button.

Normally only one measurement should be selected, however when a comparison between the different measurements is needed, up to 4 additional measurements can be selected and loaded in the same way.

To remove a selected measurement, press the Remove Measurement button.

To confirm the final selection and view the measured data press the Accept button.
